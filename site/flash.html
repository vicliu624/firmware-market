Web Flash (multi-device)

Flash firmware from your browser.

Choose your device family first. The flasher will match the correct protocol (ESP32 esptool, RP2040 UF2 drag, or STM32 DFU WebUSB).

Download firmware

ESP32 (esptool-js)

  1. Put your device in bootloader mode.
  2. Click Connect and select the serial port.
  3. Start flashing and keep the device connected.

RP2040 (UF2 drag)

  1. Hold BOOTSEL and plug in the device to mount it as a drive.
  2. Download the UF2 file and drag it to the device drive.

STM32 (DFU WebUSB)

  1. Put the device into DFU mode.
  2. Connect over USB and start flashing.
